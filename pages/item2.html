Battleship Game

Play Battleship against a CPU opponent! Be the first to sink all of your opponent's ships to win!

Rules to the Game

About Battleship
- Before play begins, you must arrange your ships on your primary grid.
- Each ship occupies a number of consecutive squares on the grid, arranged either horizontally or vertically.
- The number of squares for each ship is determined by the type of ship.
- The ships cannot overlap and the types and numbers of ships allowed are the same for each player.
How to Play
- After the ships have been positioned, the game proceeds in a series of rounds.
- In each round, each player selects a target square to shoot at.
- If an enemy ship is occupying that square, that square will turn red. If no enemy ship is occupying that square, the square will receive an X.
- When all of the squares of a ship have been hit, those squares will turn dark red.
- If all of a player's ships have been sunk, the game is over and their opponent wins. If all ships of both players are sunk by the end of the round, the game is a draw.
